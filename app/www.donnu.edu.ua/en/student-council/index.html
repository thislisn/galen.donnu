Student Council

At Donetsk National University named after Vasyl Stus, student self-government is actively developing, which is a good platform for student development and gaining management experience.

Student council is an opportunity to participate directly in management

  • Student Council
  • Elections to Student Self-Government Bodies

university. Student self-government representatives are included in many institutions of higher education: the Rectorate, the Academic Council, the Conference of the Labor Collective, the Scholarship Commissions and so on. Every student who has the desire and strength can join in making important decisions that influence the functioning and development of Vasyl Stus DonNU.

An integral part of the work of the Student Council is the human rights function. Pursuant to the Law on Higher Education, in agreement with the Student Council, scholarships, remittances, transfer of students, settlements to dormitories, etc. are held. Therefore, every student has the right to contact representatives of self-government if their rights are violated and the situation will be resolved.

Participation in the work of the Student Council is a huge plus to the CV. Today, Ukraine seeks to become part of the European Union, and in Europe, 90% of students who were members of student government are invited to work for leading companies. After all, their managers can be confident – new employees are able to make decisions and be responsible for them.

Participation in the work of the Student Council opens opportunities for exchanging experience with universities in other cities of Ukraine and abroad. The International Student Council Department, together with the Vasyl Stus International Donetsk National University, launches the Buddy System project aimed at adapting foreign students and integrating them into the university family. This is a good opportunity for our students to get acquainted with the different cultures of the world and to improve their level of spoken English.

So, if you have ambitions and aspirations for self-development, the Student Council of Donetsk National University named after Vasyl Stus will open the door to a world of great opportunities.
